Local SEO

(Local Search Engine Optimization)

Local Search is just like any other search on Google with the exception that it uses a different set of ranking factors or algorithms to rank in the local search results or the "near me", "nearby", and search.

In 2019, 46% of all GOOGLE Searches are looking for local information! Meaning, if your website is not optimized for Local Search, then you are most likely missing out on potential customers. With that being said, 28% of searches that include local intent will result in a purchase.

With a Local Heat Map added to your local SEO, you can watch how your business shows up in search results, analyze how visitors browse your website, and what attracts or distracts them. You can now understand why users click what they click - or don't click.

Organic SEO

( Search Engine Optimization )

An Organically SEO'd friendly site means Google and other search engines, can crawl your custom designed website efficiently, interpret the content effectively, and index it in their database. Once indexed, they can serve the most relevant and valuable web pages to their users, based on the topics searched for.

An advantage of a customized website with organic SEO, is it's "structured data". This assist's the SERP's by presenting more information in the form of "rich snippets". You not only improve the experience for the search bots but also the UX. This improves the SERP's with accuracy and more information for the search engines to resolve queries faster.

To aid in the Organic Ranking of your website, we will submit it to the most authoritative online directories and search engines, to increase your ranking and awareness on what is known as "The Map Pack".
